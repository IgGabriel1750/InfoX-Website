What we do

Peer-led support, from the airport to your first flat.

Student InfoX Hub is a community and support network built by African students already in the UK, for the students arriving next. We're the friend waiting at arrivals, the person who reads your tenancy before you sign, and the group chat that answers at 2am when something goes wrong.

A hand-picked network of trusted agents and student ambassadors. People who made the same move, recently enough to remember how it felt.

  1. 1

    Fill the Form

    Tell us what you need and when you're arriving.

    A human reads every request, usually within 24 hours.

  3. 3

    Approval Sent

    You get a confirmation and the payment details.

  4. 4

  5. 5
