Accommodation

Finding somewhere safe to live.

The honest playbook for finding housing in the UK without getting scammed in the process.

Most UK universities use the IASL (International Accommodation & Support) system or a partner portal to help students find housing. These are a decent starting point, but they aren't the whole story. The majority of affordable options live on SpareRoom and Rightmove, two sites you should bookmark before you even fly.

Filter aggressively by city zone, price, and bills-included status. Always cross-check a listing against multiple sites. Scam listings usually get reposted across several platforms with slightly different photos.

Red flags to never ignore

  • Never pay deposits or holding fees via WhatsApp, Western Union, or crypto
  • Always verify the landlord's identity and the property address independently
  • Always request the EPC before signing
  • If the landlord refuses a video tour, assume it's a scam
  • If the price is meaningfully below market, it almost always is a scam
